French Fries Recipe

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • oil
  • water
  • salt
  • chilli powder

Cooking Steps

  1. wash the potato and peel it
  2. Cut into slices
  3. Mix with salt and chilli powder
  4. Heat the oil and fry the potatoes
